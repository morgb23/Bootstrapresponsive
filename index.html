About us

El Charro, a beloved Mexican restaurant in Harrisonburg, VA, has been serving authentic Mexican cuisine with a warm and welcoming atmosphere since it opened its doors. Known for its vibrant flavors and traditional recipes, El Charro offers a variety of classic dishes, from sizzling fajitas and savory enchiladas to refreshing margaritas. With a commitment to quality ingredients and exceptional service, El Charro has become a local favorite for family gatherings, casual dining, and special celebrations. Come experience the true taste of Mexico in the heart of the Shenandoah Valley at El Charro!

Menu

Our menu balances traditional Mexican flavors with diverse options to suit every taste, perfect for any occasion!

Appetizers

  • Guacamole Dip – Freshly made with ripe avocados, tomatoes, onions, and lime. Served with tortilla chips.
  • Queso Fundido – Melted cheese with chorizo, onions, and peppers. Served with warm tortillas.
  • Nachos El Charro – Tortilla chips topped with beans, melted cheese, jalapeños, tomatoes, sour cream, and guacamole. Add chicken, beef, or steak.
  • Ceviche – Marinated shrimp and fish with lime juice, tomatoes, onions, cilantro, and avocado slices.

Salads and soups

  • Taco Salad – A crispy tortilla shell filled with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, sour cream, and guacamole. Choice of beef, chicken, or shrimp.
  • Chicken Tortilla Soup – A traditional Mexican soup with shredded chicken, tortilla strips, avocado, and a squeeze of lime.

Main dishes

  • Fajitas – Sizzling hot fajitas with your choice of chicken, steak, shrimp, or a combination, served with grilled peppers, onions, rice, beans, guacamole, sour cream, and warm tortillas.
  • Enchiladas Supreme – Four enchiladas: one chicken, one beef, one cheese, and one bean, topped with lettuce, tomatoes, and sour cream.
  • Carne Asada – Tender grilled steak served with rice, beans, guacamole, pico de gallo, and warm tortillas.
  • Pollo a la Crema – Grilled chicken breast cooked in a creamy sauce with mushrooms and onions, served with rice and beans.

Sides

  • Rice
  • Beans
  • Tortillas
  • Guacamole
  • Sour cream

Desserts

  • Churros – Fried pastry sticks dusted with cinnamon sugar and served with caramel dipping sauce.
  • Flan – A rich and creamy Mexican custard topped with caramel sauce.
  • Fried Ice Cream – Vanilla ice cream coated in a crispy shell, drizzled with chocolate and honey.

Drinks

  • Margaritas – Classic, strawberry, mango, or frozen, with salt or sugar rim.
  • Mexican Beers – Corona, Modelo, Pacifico, Dos Equis, and more.
  • Jarritos – Traditional Mexican sodas in various flavors.
  • Agua Frescas – Refreshing fruit drinks like horchata, tamarind, and hibiscus.
  • Soft Drinks – Coke, Diet Coke, Sprite, and more.
  • Water – It's just water
